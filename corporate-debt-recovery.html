Recovering a Debt

Where money is owed to you, early advice can make all the difference.

We can assist with:

  • Reviewing whether the debt should be pursued
  • Assessing the strength of your case
  • Advising on the quickest and most cost-effective route to recovery
  • Preparing letters before action and formal demands
  • Advising on interest, costs and contractual rights
  • Evaluating matters further where payment is still not made

In many cases, a carefully drafted letter from a barrister-led service can resolve the issue without the need for proceedings.

Where further action is required, we can advise on the appropriate civil or commercial routes, including the use of courts and winding up petitions where suitable.

Responding to a Demand

Not every demand for payment is properly made.

If you have received a demand for payment, we can advise you — before you respond, pay, dispute or make any admissions.

We can assess where:

  • the debt is disputed
  • there is a right of set-off
  • the amount claimed is incorrect
  • contra accounts have not been applied
  • a debt collection agency is applying inappropriate pressure
  • a statutory demand has been threatened

Debt recovery rules are specific and often misunderstood. We can help you understand your rights, respond correctly and avoid inappropriate pressure.

Better Credit Control

Good debt recovery starts before the debt arises.

We can help businesses design a practical credit control process that protects cash flow while preserving relationships with customers and clients.

This may include:

  • Scripted reminder processes
  • Staged letter templates and formal paper
  • Terms of business and payment clauses
  • Escalation and instructional clauses
  • Guidance on when to pause
  • Check-in reviews
  • Advice on when legal intervention is appropriate and lawful

The aim is simple: to give your business a consistent and professional process for managing payment issues early.

Our Philosophy

A Human Approach to Recovery

Debt recovery does not have to be aggressive to be effective.

We balance the commercial need to secure what is owed by combining legal pressure with commercial judgment.

Some cases require a firm and immediate response. Others require a measured approach that preserves relationships and opens a dialogue.

Our role is to help you recover what is owed, protect your position and avoid unnecessary escalation wherever possible.

Whether you are seeking payment, resisting a demand, or considering whether a debt should be pursued or paid, we can provide clear, practical advice at an early stage.
