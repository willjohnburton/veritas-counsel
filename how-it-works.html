How It Works

Elite counsel, made simple

No gatekeepers, no surprise bills. You gain direct access to senior barristers from day one — with transparent fixed fees and a clear plan at every step.

Five steps to protection

1

Get in touch

Book a confidential 15 or 30 minute call, or send an enquiry. Tell us about your matter — there is no charge for this first conversation.

2

Confidential discussion

You speak directly with a senior barrister who understands how disputes develop and how to prevent them — not a paralegal or trainee.

3

Strategy & fixed fee

We set out the strengths, the risks and a clear plan. You receive transparent, predictable pricing up front — you always know the cost before we proceed.

4

Decisive action

We act — advising, negotiating and, where needed, representing you with the full weight of our courtroom capability behind you.

5

Ongoing protection

Through our programmes we stay alongside you — pre-empting problems before they arise and protecting your people, your business and your future.
