Individual Protection

Engage early. Protect yourself.

We offer a no fee conversation over the phone or in person to discuss your matter.

You'll receive transparent, clear and simple advice. We give you the strengths and weaknesses of your case at the outset, based on the information you provide.

If you wish to instruct us, we offer:

  • A clear path
  • Straightforward advice ("no nonsense") – we tell you the good and the bad (strengths and weaknesses)
  • Keep you in full control – we provide you drafts of each step
  • Set out each step at the outset
  • Give an overview on chances of success
  • Never deviate from our mantra
  • Set out fixed fees so you can pick and choose when to stop, or whether to even start
  • Set out a strategy from the beginning
  • Offer no win no fees on some cases
  • Potentially offer insurance backed introductions for before the event or after the event insurance (to protect against litigation risk) which we explain in clear language if your case needs it.
